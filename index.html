I received my Ph.D. degree in the Key Laboratory of Aerospace Information Security and Trusted Computing at Wuhan University, China, advised by Prof. Lina Wang. Before that, I received my M.Sc. degree majoring in Software Engineering at Wuhan University.

My current research interests are mainly about network security and AI security. Specifically, I focus on the security issues on the deep learning models. Also, I am very interested in the application and security issues of generative artificial intelligence, as well as multimodal learning.

I actively serve as a reviewer for numerous prestigious conferences and journals, including ESWA, Artificial Intelligence Review, and IJCNN, among others.
